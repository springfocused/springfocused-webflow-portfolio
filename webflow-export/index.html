01 — WHAT  I  DO
01   WHAT  I  DO
01   WHAT  I  DO
Stories before visuals.
I create photo and video work with a strong focus on storytelling.
From the first idea to the final frame, my work connects concept, production and thoughtful collaboration.
02 — CONCEPTION
02   CONCEPTION
02   CONCEPTION
Nothing starts without intent.
Every project starts with a clear idea.
I don’t approach photography or film by simply pressing record — I take time to understand the context, the people involved and what the story needs.
Conception means asking the right questions before making visual decisions.
From mood and narrative to rhythm, location and light, the groundwork shapes everything that follows.
03 — PRODUCTION
03   PRODUCTION
03   PRODUCTION
Craft over complexity.
Production is where ideas become tangible.
I take care of the full execution — from camera, light and sound to directing, timing and on-set decisions.
My focus stays on clarity and intention throughout the shoot.
Every choice serves the story, not the setup.
